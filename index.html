Automating doctor appointments
through WhatsApp voice.

Patients simply send a WhatsApp voice note. Behind the scenes, an AI agent orchestrated by n8n uses OpenAI, ElevenLabs, Google Calendar and a Node.js backend to understand intent, propose slots, confirm, and log appointments — end-to-end.

From audio → transcript → intent → schedule in under 10 seconds.
Focused strictly on scheduling, no medical advice.

End-to-end journey (demo-friendly)
From patient voice to booked slot
1 Patient sends a WhatsApp voice note describing date, time & reason.
2 n8n + OpenAI transcribe the audio, extract intent & constraints.
3 Node.js backend checks Google Calendar, proposes optimal slots, and logs them.
4 ElevenLabs + Twilio send back a synthetic voice + text confirmation to the patient.
